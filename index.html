Welcome to Physiohab

Dr. Eesha Talawadekar is a highly experienced physiotherapist dedicated to personalized and effective care.

With years of clinical experience, Dr. Eesha specializes in orthopedic, neurological, and sports-related physiotherapy. She blends modern techniques with compassionate care to ensure every patient achieves their best physical health. Her expertise also includes dry and wet needling, cupping therapy, and post-operative rehabilitation.

Back Pain Therapy

Neck & Shoulder Pain

Knee Rehabilitation

Cupping Therapy

Traditional technique for muscle relaxation and improved blood flow.

Ultrasound Therapy

Non-invasive deep tissue therapy to accelerate healing and reduce pain.
